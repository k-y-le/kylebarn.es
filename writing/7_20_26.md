i have taken eight pictures in the month of july. so few pixel arrangements to tether to. but such a rich season. learning, growing, new friends new explorations. so much (perhaps too much?) to look forward to.

feeling a bit overwhelmed. trying to fit everything in before i go to the mountains and splay myself atop pikes peak. do you want to come to my event? do you want to come? i am feeling a tad desperate. 

keep worrying that i’m going to get the parasite but i just really love eating my salads. keep saying “ooh i need to do this” but not doing it. because there’s not enough time!!! the thing they never tell you about creative actualization is that you will run out of time for every project. we need an artist’s way but just for prioritization. 

am i creatively actualized? honestly, yes, i’m a fucking creative force in this world. that’s something i’ve been saying to myself, when the doubt creeps in. i can do anything and i will. i’ll give a lecture before a play and i’ll make a zine half asleep sunday night and i’ll write for that magazine and i’ll update that website and i’ll run around and around and around again.

i am going to eat the salad and get the parasite. i am going to sage the iphone and get called a brooklyn caricature. i am going to text back two weeks late with an invite to my next event (or three). i am going to the grocery store to buy the salad that will give me the parasite. i am still really worried about climate change, by the way.
