group hug

ingredients: 2+ people and a public location

pick a particular time and central location for a one-minute long group hug. tell participants to come at that time. hug for exactly one minute. disperse.

caution labels

ingredients: label maker and a place to walk

bring a label maker on a walk. print out caution statements and place them on random objects as you go. cautions should be absurd, vaguely philosophical, and unrelated to the objects placed upon.

ex: "CAUTION: MEANING IS INSUFFICIENT", "CAUTION: BEGIN TO FORGET YOURSELF", "CAUTION: OPEN UP TO COLLECTIVE INTELLIGENCE"

5 minute public support

ingredients: 2+ people who could use some support right now and a public location

meet at a public location. lean on each other until each participant is dependent on the support of the others. after 5 minutes, disperse.

group sway

ingredients: 3+ people and a place to walk

start walking. as you go, watch one another and collectively adopt a swaying motion. do not verbally discuss your sway. go until you reach your destination.

handshake knot

ingredients: 3+ people with hands

form a circle. shake each other's hands and twist your arms until you form a knot. sporadically let go and keep forming new knots until you know each other.

tiny relay

ingredients: 4+ people and a place to run

split into teams. hold a relay race in a loop that is under 10 feet in diameter. use a found object as a baton. when one team wins, celebrate.

______ exchange

ingredients: an agreed upon object to exchange and a public place

pick an object that means something to you, but not a commodity. tell your friends that you are holding an exchange. in a central location, present your objects and discuss until each person leaves with a different object than they came with.

ex: leaf exchange, motivation exchange, caress exchange

go west

ingredients: 3+ people

at one particular time, starting from different locations, go west. when all participants have arrived west, go home.

highest five

ingredients: 3+ people of different heights and wingspans

the tallest person raises their hand as high as they can. for one minute, all other participants attempt to high five that hand.

quench

ingredients: 3+ people, a public location, and some water

all participants arrive at the public location at a set time, very thirsty. participants take turns providing each other with water until all are quenched.

rest your weary legs

ingredients: 3+ people with legs

take turns carrying one another until all feel well rested.

whose weeds

ingredients: 3+ people and some weeds

all participants bring a weed to a particular location. make a bouquet.

shadow money

ingredients: $1 bills and some pretty cool shadows

find a cool shadow. tape or leave a $1 bill on the shadow. find another cool shadow and repeat until you are out of money.
