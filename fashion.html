on march 7, 2019, i exhibited original designs at the princeton university art museum. i was selected as a student designer and stylist by the undergraduate student body, and received financial support to create my own looks based off pieces in the art museum. my looks represent an abstraction of jean-michel basquiat's piece Poison Oasis, inspired by the symbolism in his work, as well as the close relationship of personal identity and artistic expression in the form of clothing.
