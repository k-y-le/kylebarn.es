i'm kyle, an artist-researcher trying to live well on a damaged planet.

i use community and computing as creative mediums, seeding playful ritual gatherings. i facilitate, write, design, draw, dj, bake, paint, study, strategize, teach, collaborate, build, destroy, dance, and spend a lot of time upside down.

i'm one half of chimeras collective, hosting interspecies gatherings and classes that re-enchant the urban ecologies of nyc. and i'm one half of tomorrow soup, creating alternative social and digital spaces for communal care, queer joy, and climate grief.
